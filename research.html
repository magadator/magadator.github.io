DNS of Pulsatile Flow in Curved Pipes

Study of turbulence development in pulsatile flow under Dean curvature effects. Focus on vortex breakdown, secondary flow structures, and transition mechanisms.

Aortic Dissection CFD

Patient-specific simulations using CT/MRI reconstructions. Analysis of wall shear stress (WSS), oscillatory shear index (OSI), and flow separation.
